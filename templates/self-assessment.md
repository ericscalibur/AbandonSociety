### 5.2 Readiness Level Interpretation

**90-100 points: High Readiness**
You have excellent resources, skills, and support for rapid transition. Consider:
- Large-scale integrated projects
- Community leadership roles
- Mentoring others in your network
- Advanced experimental techniques
- Multiple domain simultaneous development

**70-89 points: Good Readiness**
You have solid foundation for significant progress. Focus on:
- 2-3 priority domains simultaneously
- Building community connections
- Intermediate skill development
- Medium-scale projects with measurable outcomes
- Sharing knowledge with others

**50-69 points: Moderate Readiness**
You have basic foundation but need focused development. Priorities:
- Choose 1-2 primary domains for intensive focus
- Skill building through courses and mentorship
- Small-scale pilot projects
- Building support network
- Addressing major constraints

**30-49 points: Limited Readiness**
Significant preparation needed before major projects. Start with:
- Basic skill development in highest priority domain
- Financial planning and resource building
- Networking and community connections
- Small experiments and learning projects
- Addressing fundamental constraints

**Below 30 points: Early Preparation**
Focus on foundation building before implementation:
- Education and skill development
- Financial stability and resource accumulation
- Building support networks
- Addressing major obstacles
- Long-term planning and vision development
